Frequently Asked Questions


IIT Ropar stands out for several reasons that contribute to its unique identity among the IITs:

AI for All: No matter what branch you came from, we at IIT Ropar give you the opportunity to get a minor in AI. So, whether you’re a future electrical engineer or a budding biotechnologist, AI can be your sidekick.

Branch Change: Unlike many other IITs, IIT Ropar offers a branch change option at the end of the 1st year. Didn’t get the branch of your choice? Worry not, we’ve got you covered. You can choose the branch of your dreams even after the 1st year. It's like a second chance at love, but with engineering.

Global Collaborations: We participate in international research projects and exchange programs. Because why limit your genius to one country?

6-Month Internship Opportunity: Unlike other IITs, we offer a 6-month internship opportunity for 3rd-year students. Dive deep into the industry world and take your time deciding on your dream career. It’s like a career buffet, and you get to taste everything before committing.

Location, Location, Location: At the end of the day, you want a place to live peacefully and breathe nicely. Located in the land of five rivers, where greenery and fresh air bloom, our lush green campus offers you a peaceful life. Plus, Chandigarh is just a hop, skip, and a jump away, and the railway station is only 6-7 km from here. Easy connectivity means you’re never far from the action—or a weekend getaway.

New Buildings and Hostels: As a growing IIT, we have brand new, beautiful buildings, iconic structures, and modern hostels. It’s like living in an architectural wonderland.

In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape, we are witnessing a significant boom in Artificial Intelligence (AI). While there are speculations that AI might replace certain jobs, we strongly believe that AI will also create numerous new opportunities and job roles that we can't even imagine today. AI has the potential to streamline and enhance various aspects of our work and daily life.

To ensure our students are well-prepared for this future, we are offering a minor in "AI for All." This program will provide you with a foundational understanding of AI concepts and practical applications. By gaining this knowledge, you'll be better equipped to navigate and thrive in a world where AI plays a central role, positioning yourself at the forefront of technological innovation and career opportunities.
